Civil Services

The term generally refers to administrator paid for incrementing the policy of National Government. The Civil Service is one of the most prestigious examination in India and it is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission(UPSC) The three types of sevices recruited through the Civil Services examinations are All India Services, Central Services and State Services.

 List of Civil Services for which combined examinations are held:

  2. Indian Foreign Services
